About

It all began with a straightforward idea to create an exceptional T-shirt. a clothing that wicked away perspiration from your skin instead of absorbing it. A clothing that improved performance and regulated temperature in concert with your body. Kevin Plank, a former football player for the University of Maryland, founded Under Armour in 1996. The company is known for creating performance clothing, which is designed to keep athletes light, dry, and cool during practice, competition, or training. The strategy to profit from Under Armour's wide range of products for men, women, and kids is straightforward: wear HeatGear in the heat, AllSeasonGear in the cold, and other gear between the extremes. The technology underlying these products is sophisticated.

Making every athlete better through passion, design, and unwavering innovation pursuit is Under Armour's objective.
