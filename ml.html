About

Milky Lane has become a household name in fabulously fun ice-cream delights and the most famous waffles.

Loved by friends and family, the key ingredient in a variety of decadent items including wonderfilled waffles, sunnydaes, milkyshakes, and delicious parfaits is NiceCream, a premium soft serve.

Remember the incredible Whizzer, a one-of-a-kind treat that combines NiceCream with chocolate, almonds, fruit, and confections?

It is in Milky Lane that you can indulge your imagination and feel cozy and comforted.

Garnished with a delectable assortment of toppings and flavors, and served with a double order of smiles.
