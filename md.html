About

Founded in 1996, My Diners was a modest yet ambitious takeout restaurant offering the greatest Tikka in the nation. With the help of our devoted patrons, we have expanded into a sizable chain of family restaurants. We provide the greatest assortment of vegetarian, vegan, Chinese, Pakistani, Italian, seafood, and Western dishes. We serve delicious sizzlers and the freshest, most delicious naan. Additionally, we provide a large assortment of delectable homemade ice cream.
