About

We don't have much time left to appreciate nice, traditional customs and values because we live in a fast-paced modern world. This holds true for both food and dining customs.

For this reason, at Barcelos, we always work to strike a balance between the fast-lane demands of the modern world and traditional, healthful home-style meals. Our fast-food establishments specialize in serving flame-grilled chicken that is both spicy and healthful, made with the same authenticity as the ancient Portuguese chefs. With its roots in history, culture, and tradition, Barcelos now provides a convenient and laid-back dining experience for people from many different backgrounds.
