About

An official hub for Apple goods and accessories is called iStore, an Apple Premium Reseller (APR). With 22 locations conveniently positioned across the country, iStore is the largest APR in Southern Africa. By giving customers a real Apple experience and delivering special services like in-store free training, technical assistance, and technical setup, iStore hopes to maximize customer value offerings. iStore provides 2-year extended warranty options with iCare Plus in addition to contracts on multiple mobile networks.
