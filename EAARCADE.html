About

For everyone, it is an adrenaline rush. R2 and up are the games we offer. We serve both adults and small children. We provide a pleasant and safe atmosphere. A few of our activities provide the younger ones with tickets that they can use to enter our toy shop and win prizes. Our employees are kind and accommodating. We provide party packages. Depending on how busy we are, we open at 9:00 a.m. and close at 9:00 p.m. on school holidays.

Action bowling is age- and gender-neutral and fun for everybody. We provide birthday and corporate packages. It is enjoyable and safe here. Our team members are always willing to assist and have a great time. We support up to eight players per lane and have 14 lanes. Printouts with the scores are available, but you have to request them before you play.

For all ages, Formula Dodgems is an exhilarating experience. You are welcome to bring friends or family with you. We drive more than eight vehicles. Each thrilling ride lasts for around four minutes. We have a friendly staff and a lively atmosphere. Packages for birthdays are available.
