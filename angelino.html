About

When the Angelino brand was first launched in 1999, it was meant to represent sophistication, choice, and style for the guy who expected nothing less than the finest in modern men's fashion. In 2008, a product that started out in the neck tie business as a fabric-focused product that was produced and imported for the South African retail market progressively advanced to a larger variety of menswear, including shirts, jeans, shoes, waistcoats, belts, ties, and cuff links.
