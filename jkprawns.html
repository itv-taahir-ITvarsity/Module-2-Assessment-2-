About

Jimmy's began many moons ago, in 1991, when its founder, Jimmy, who loved prawns, decided to honor them. In Johannesburg, South Africa, Jimmys launched his first restaurant with a modest setup, no money, and a dream. Jimmy started serving his first customers out of an old mining home with a grill and a container freezer.

a straightforward dish of bread and delicately flavored prawns on the grill. Jimmy's became the talk of the town in just a few months. People would travel for hours in order to enjoy and taste the prawn.

In less than a year, Jimmy's established his first physical location and increased the variety of prawn dishes he offered with heated tastes from his trips throughout the world. Jimmys is now a worldwide chain of restaurants with locations in Europe, the Middle East, and Southern Africa, with more to open soon.
