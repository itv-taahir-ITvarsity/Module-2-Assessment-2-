ABOUT US

Welcome to Pine City Mall, the ultimate shopping, dining, and entertainment destination in the heart of the city! Since opening our doors, Pine City Mall has become a favorite spot for locals and visitors alike, offering a unique blend of top brands, diverse culinary experiences, and a wide range of entertainment options for all ages.

Our mall is designed to provide a one-of-a-kind experience, featuring spacious walkways, elegant interiors, and a welcoming atmosphere. With over 90 stores, we bring together international and local brands to cater to all your shopping needs. Whether you’re on the hunt for the latest fashion trends, tech gadgets, home décor, or simply looking for a place to unwind, Pine City Mall has something for everyone.
