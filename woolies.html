About

In October 1931, the first Woolworths store in Cape Town opened for business. And it was founder Max Sonnenberg who distinguished Woolworths from its rivals by capturing the public's attention with innovative shop strategies.

A second branch opened in Durban after three years, and two more in Port Elizabeth and Johannesburg the following year. Since then, we have worked to further establish our reputation for outstanding value, thrilling innovation, and top quality.
