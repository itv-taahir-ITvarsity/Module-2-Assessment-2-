About

With the best elements of squash and tennis combined, padel is a dynamic sport that offers more enjoyment, excitement, and social interaction.

The Virgin Active Padel Clubs are accessible to anyone. Therefore, you will still be able to reserve courts, purchase and rent items, use the restroom and locker room facilities, and enjoy the food and drink options even if you are not a member.
