About

In South Africa, the Young Designers Emporium (YDE) is leading the way in spotting and nurturing homegrown designers. The purpose of YDE is to exhibit the artistic abilities of emerging South African designers of apparel, accessories, and footwear.

We provide up-and-coming designers a well-established venue on which to display and market their custom goods. Our unique, seductive, and expressive clothing and accessory lines and brands appeal to a young, fashionable clientele that keeps a close eye on both domestic and global fashion trends.
