About

Gloria Jeans Coffees is a retail coffee brand based in Australia that runs over a thousand locations across 39 countries.

The franchise, which is owned by Retail Food Holdings (RFH), a company located in South Africa, presently operates three coffee shops. In 2020 and 2021, it plans to open more locations, bringing Gloria Jeans Coffee's excellent taste and experience to every region of the country.
