About

The best seafood restaurant in South Africa, Ocean Basket is dedicated to providing you with mouthwatering seafood and a cozy, homey atmosphere that will make you long for the days when you ate dinner with your loved ones at the family table. From prawns, sushi, and calamari to our well-known fish and chips and, of course, our renowned creamy lemon sauce, we provide a variety of Mediterranean-inspired cuisine. Come to any of our eateries to share one of our generous platters, indulge in our complimentary bread, or dunk your prawn into one of our three sauces.
