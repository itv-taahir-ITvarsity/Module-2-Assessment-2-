About

We are pleased to provide a distinctive Thai dining experience at Simply Asia, with food cooked by our chefs and a menu full of delectable options to suit a variety of palates. And it is no surprise that we are South Africa's favorite Thai restaurant—with more than 50 locations across the Western Cape, Eastern Cape, Gauteng, and KwaZulu-Natal, as well as our expanding footprint in Botswana and Zimbabwe—and our award-winning cuisine.

The food at Simply Asia is consistently of the highest quality and flavor, regardless of the store you visit, thanks to meticulous quality control inspections conducted by our head chefs and founder Chai Lekcharoensuk himself on all of our ingredients before they are delivered to your table.
