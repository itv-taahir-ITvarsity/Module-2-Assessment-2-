About

We think the story of the LEGO Groups—that's us!—is a very sweet and straightforward one. Ole Kirk Kristiansen, our founder, was a skilled joiner and carpenter. He started producing toys for sale in Billund, Denmark, in 1932, and that's when the LEGO brand was established!

The renowned LEGO Brick didn't initially surface until somewhat later in the 1950s. Since then, we’ve offered diverse toys, play experiences and instructional resources to over 130 countries.

The Danish terms "leg godt," which translate to "play well," are combined to form the name LEGO. It is both our name and our ideal.
