About

Calistos Portuguese Restaurant was established in 1992 by a creative and accomplished couple who have a deep love for Portuguese cuisine and flavors. Portuguese cuisine enthusiasts have come to associate the robust family brand with its modest origins 24 years ago, and it has expanded enormously since then.

With dishes freshly made in the traditional Portuguese style using only the best, freshest ingredients available, the brand has become well-known for its extensive menu, which features everything from the best flame-grilled Portuguese chicken to the greatest fish and pork. You will have a pleasant experience at these restaurants that will entice you to return time and time again because of their genuine character and lively, energetic atmosphere.

It is the greatest experience south of Portugal and more than just a restaurant.
