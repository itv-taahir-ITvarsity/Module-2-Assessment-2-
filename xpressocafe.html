About

Hello, our names are Nicolene and Tomer Elhadad, and we are the happy owners of the South African Xpresso Cafe. Our ambition has always been to launch a coffee service that is accessible to anyone.

Our goal was to provide customers to excellent coffee at the most competitive price. Why, then, R10? Sincerely, why not? Giving has always been preferable to receiving in life; things do not have to be costly, and sure, we do not make a lot of money.
