About

The majority of South Africans associate the word "movies" with the brand Ster-Kinekor. As the largest movie exhibitor—more than twice as large as its closest rival—Ster-Kinekor holds between 60 and 65 percent of the market. It has approximately 55 movie complexes in the country (with over 400 screens and 64 000 seats), including 154 state-of-the-art 3D theaters, giving it the largest 3D footprint in the region. It also has six complexes in neighboring territories. For show times, please see the Ster-Kinekor website.
