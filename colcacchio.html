About

Col'Cacchio is a classy Italian pizzeria that opened in 1992. Our dedication to health is as renowned as our gourmet pizzas, and we guarantee our guests a fun, healthy, and enjoyable dining experience with high-quality food. We specialize on Italian-inspired cuisine, which includes a wide array of freshly made salads and pastas as well as crisp, thin-crust pizzas.
