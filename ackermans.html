About

In 1916, Gus Ackerman inaugurated the first Ackermans store in Wynberg, Cape Town, marking the beginning of our story. His goal was to make high-quality goods accessible to all. At the core of each and every one of the more than 740 Ackermans stores that exist today across five countries, this is their goal.

We are aware that your family has always come first. Paying less shouldn't have to mean sacrificing style or quality in order to free up money for life's essentials. Award-winning quality products at incomparable costs are the first step, but there are also fun promotions, convenient shop access, and helpful service.
