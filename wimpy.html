About

Wimpy arrived in South Africa in the same year that Priscilla and Elvis got married, Coca-Cola became 10 cents, Aretha Franklin's Respect topped the Billboard Charts, and the Beatles Sgt Pepper's Lonely Hearts Club Band won the Grammy for Album of the Year.

Since its founding in 1967 in Murchies Passage, Durban, 50 years have passed, and Wimpy has firmly established itself as a South African icon. With several new stores in the works, our network currently has over 500 franchised locations in South Africa, Botswana, Namibia, and Zambia.

We serve families from all walks of life; our 11 SANHA-accredited Halaal stores around the country guarantee that our customers can always trust our cuisine, and our Playword kids' play facilities keep the little ones occupied.

With our constantly expanding network of locations, there is always a Wimpy close by where you can Enjoy Every Moment.
