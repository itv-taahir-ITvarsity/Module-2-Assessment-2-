About

Spur Steak Ranches have been a part of the South African family since its founding, in 1967, when Allen Ambor launched the Golden Spur in Newlands, Cape Town.

Since then, Spur has contributed to the making of innumerable priceless moments and established a solid reputation for providing good, wholesome, and affordable meals.

Along with numerous local and foreign eateries, we have developed into a globally recognized brand.

What is our secret to success, then? It is simple: large servings of delicious food, a substantial dose of quality, and a cozy, laid-back atmosphere that welcomes families!
