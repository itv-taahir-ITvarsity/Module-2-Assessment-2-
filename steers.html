About

For more than 50 years, Steers has been the top flame-grilled burger brand in South Africa, providing its customers with authentic, high-quality meals. The fact that we have received the Leisure Options Readers' Choice Award for best burger for 15 years running and best chips for 13 years running is proof positive of our delicious food.

Steers is known for its handcrafted chips and burgers. Along with the popular king cow burger and a variety of other well-known favorites like our flame-grilled basted ribs, hero steak rolls, creamy ice cream, and milkshakes, our menu offers much more than just burgers made of beef, chicken, or vegetarian options.

To create the greatest burgers in town, we only employ the freshest ingredients in traditional flavor combinations. Almost everything we serve is prepared fresh. Our entire operation revolves around the burger, which is always entirely authentic.
